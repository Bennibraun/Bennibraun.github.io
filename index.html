About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. I work at the intersection of computing and biology with a focus on software for genomics and bioinformatics. I am interested in developing tools to help biologists and clinicians analyze and interpret large-scale biological data.
